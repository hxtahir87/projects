About

We have a simple manifesto, "Provide deep insights to underground storage tank operators so, they can maximize their margins while verifying for any leaks."

Tanklytics was born out of necessity for ourselves. EPA and State agencies have strict inventory control requirements to ensure underground storage tanks are not leaking and contaminating the environment. As part of these requirements, a daily inventory record is kept that tracks how many gallons have been pumped and/or delivered. By the end of the month, a leak check is performed which aims to tell operators if there is a leak.

As Gas Station operators ourselves, we tried completing these reports with spreadsheets but, we thought there has to be a better way! A way that is simpler but, provides 10x the value!

Tanklytics aims to not just help you with your EPA/State required inventory records but, provide you with deep understanding of sales. With the key insights provided by Tanklytics, you transform your business for the better.
